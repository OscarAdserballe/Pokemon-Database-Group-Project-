About Us

For millennia, man has lived alongside Pokemon in peace and prosperity, hunting together in the warm summers and snuggling against each other during the freezing winters. While each person eventually finds their complementary Pokemon, based on size, colour, type, and abilities, the most important trait one considers while choosing their companion, cuteness, has never been objectified in any Pokemon card, game, database, or cartoon. To break this trend, we have developed several algorithms relating the creatures’ characteristics with an empirical measurement of their cuteness.
